The Developing Countries and Fairtrade

Developing countries, predominantly in Africa, South America, and Asia, are major contributors to Fairtrade products. By supporting Fairtrade, you contribute to empowering farmers and workers in these regions.

Where to Find Fairtrade Products

You can support Fairtrade anywhere, from your local supermarkets like Asda, Tesco, and CO-OP to online platforms like Amazon. Fairtrade is not limited to food; it includes clothing, jewelry, makeup, art, and even home decor.

Fairtrade Companies to Look Out For:

  • Cocoa Loco
  • Divine Chocolate
  • Ben & Jerry's
  • Cadbury's (No longer part)
  • Unreal
  • Lidl's Branded Chocolate Products
