Advantages and Disadvantages of Fair Trade:

Advantages:

  • Child Labour Reduced
  • Improves social conditions
  • Buyers feel happy about supporting fair products
  • Ensures workers are paid fairly
  • Ensures safe working conditions

Disadvantages:

  • Product choice is reduced
  • The product often costs more
  • Big buyers are not often attracted to Fairtrade
